Privacy Policy for Logo Quiz

1. Introduction

Welcome to Logo Quiz (“we”, “us”, “our”). We are committed to protecting your privacy. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, disclose, and safeguard your information when you use our mobile application (the “App”).

2. Information We Collect

We may collect the following types of information:

  • Device & Usage Information: Device type, operating system version, unique device identifiers, app usage statistics, crash logs, and performance data.
  • No Personal Identifying Information: We do not collect personal details such as your name, address, phone number, or email unless you choose to contact us.
  • Analytics Services (Firebase): The App uses Firebase Analytics and Firebase Crashlytics to collect anonymous usage and crash data. This helps us improve app performance and fix issues.
  • Advertising (Google AdMob): The App displays banner ads via Google AdMob. AdMob may collect device identifiers, approximate location, or ad interaction data to deliver and measure ads. No personally identifiable information is collected by us.

3. How We Use Your Information

We use the collected information to:

  • Display ads using Google AdMob.
  • Improve the App using analytics insights.
  • Fix bugs and crashes via Crashlytics.
  • Understand how users interact with different features.
  • Ensure proper functionality and security of the App.

4. Third-Party Services

The App uses the following trusted third-party services:

  • Google AdMob – for displaying banner advertisements.
  • Firebase Analytics – for anonymous usage analytics.
  • Firebase Crashlytics – for crash reporting.

Please review the privacy policies for these services:

  • Google AdMob Privacy & Ads Policy
  • Firebase Privacy & Data Collection

5. Sharing Your Information

We do not sell your personal information. Aggregated and anonymized analytics data may be shared with third-party services solely for app improvement and advertisement delivery.

6. Data Retention

We retain anonymized usage and diagnostic data only as long as necessary to operate, improve, and maintain the App, or as required by applicable laws.

7. Security

We implement reasonable administrative, technical, and physical safeguards to protect your information. However, no method of transmission or storage is 100% secure.

8. Children's Privacy

The App is suitable for users aged 4+ and does not knowingly collect personal information from children. If you believe a child has provided personal information, please contact us so we can remove it.

9. Your Rights & Choices

  • You can reset or limit advertising identifiers in your device settings.
  • You may disable personalized ads through Google settings.
  • You may request deletion of analytics data stored by Firebase (contact us).

10. Changes to This Privacy Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. Changes will be posted on this page with an updated date.
